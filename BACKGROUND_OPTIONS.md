# Background Options for Medical App

Here are professional background options for your medical application:

## Option 1: Static Gradient (Recommended) ⭐
**Clean, professional, non-distracting**
- Static gradient from blue to green
- Uses your logo colors
- No animation, just smooth color transition
- Perfect for medical/professional settings

## Option 2: Light Solid Background
**Ultra clean, minimal**
- Light color background (Swans Down #dcf0e8)
- Very subtle, professional
- Focuses attention on content
- Common in medical/clinical apps

## Option 3: Subtle Radial Gradient
**Soft, calming**
- Radial gradient from center
- Very subtle, almost static
- Creates depth without distraction
- Professional appearance

## Option 4: Two-Tone Static
**Simple, elegant**
- Two-color gradient (Ocean Green → Emerald)
- Clean and simple
- Maintains brand colors
- Minimal visual noise

## Option 5: Very Slow Animation
**Subtle movement**
- Same gradient but much slower (20-30 seconds)
- Barely noticeable movement
- Adds life without distraction
- Still professional

---

**Recommendation**: Option 1 (Static Gradient) or Option 2 (Light Solid) for maximum professionalism.
